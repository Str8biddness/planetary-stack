⚙️ Settings
Result verification
Require verified evidence
Refuse a result unless the machine that produced it signed a valid execution record. Turn this off and results are still checked and still labelled — you just are not blocked.
What a verified result means. The machine that ran the work signed a record naming the image, entrypoint, inputs and lease it used, and this desktop checked that signature against the key it recorded when the device was enrolled. It proves which machine produced the result and that the approved image and inputs were named — it does not prove that machine is honest. A compromised device holds its own key. Hardware attestation is not available yet.

🧭 Welcome Guide
MODULES

Welcome to your new operating system.

Synthesus is a private, distributed operating system built to give you total control over Artificial Intelligence. Instead of sending your data to the cloud, everything runs directly on your hardware.

No subscriptions. No data mining. Total privacy.

Whether you are a power user or completely new to AI, this system is designed to be completely intuitive. Click through the modules on the left to learn exactly how to use each feature, complete with real-world examples!

🧠 Quadbrain Chat (SI)

The Quadbrain is your core AI assistant. Think of it like a completely private ChatGPT that lives on your computer. You can ask it to write emails, solve math, or brainstorm ideas. But it also does something else: Intent Routing.

If you ask it to generate an image, it instantly understands your command and silently hands the task over to the Image Forge.

Mock Example 1 (Standard AI):
"Write a polite email to my boss asking for next Friday off."
Mock Example 2 (Intent Routing):
"draw a cyber city at sunset with neon lights"
(This automatically builds a 3D scene in the Image Forge!)

🎨 Image Forge

The Image Forge generates stunning visual scenes entirely on-device. Unlike other tools that take 30 seconds to download a picture from the cloud, the Forge mathematically builds scenes on your graphics card in milliseconds.

It uses something called "Signed Distance Fields" to instantly carve geometry out of math. You don't need an internet connection.

How to use it:
  • Open the Forge and select a Scene like "Menger Sponge" or "Gyroid lattice".
  • Drag the sliders like Smooth blend and Fractal depth to watch the world physically shift and morph in real-time.
  • Click Animate to see the math in motion.

💽 Expansion Drive

Have you ever wanted an AI to read all your personal files so you can ask it questions? The Expansion Drive lets you do exactly that—securely.

You can paste text or connect folders. The system converts your files into "knowledge vectors" and stores them on your local hard drive.

Mock Example:
1. Open the Drive and paste in a 10-page PDF of your company's employee handbook.
2. Name the drive "HR Docs" and hit Build.
3. Open the Quadbrain Chat and ask: "According to my files, how many PTO days do I get?" The AI instantly knows!

📡 System Vitals

When you use cloud software, you never really know what the company is doing with your data. System Vitals fixes that.

Vitals acts as a live heartbeat monitor for your operating system. It proves, using cryptographic signatures, exactly what your computer is doing, what network connections are active, and guarantees there is zero hidden telemetry.

How to check your privacy:
Whenever you run an AI workload or build a local drive, watch the Vitals window. You will see a live cryptographic proof confirming that the work was handled locally and securely.
Image forge

Every pixel is computed from geometry — signed distance fields combined by constructive solid geometry (union, intersection, difference), scaled by fractal folding. No bitmap, no network, no model. The image is a real evaluation of the scene, not a fabrication.
